About Kavach AI

Kavach AI Security is an advanced browser extension that employs multi-layered AI analysis to detect and neutralize phishing attempts in real-time. Our solution transcends traditional methods by incorporating sophisticated machine learning algorithms and computer vision technology to protect users from increasingly convincing fake websites.

Unlike conventional security tools that rely on outdated blacklists or simplistic detection methods, Kavach AI uses a combination of deep URL analysis, visual fingerprinting, behavioral analysis, and contextual trust scoring to provide comprehensive protection against modern phishing threats.

How It Works

When you visit a website, Kavach AI Security performs a comprehensive analysis in real-time:

  1. Data Collection: The extension collects various data points about the website, including URL structure, visual elements, page behavior, and form inputs.
  2. AI Analysis: Our advanced AI models analyze the collected data using multiple techniques, including natural language processing, computer vision, and behavioral pattern recognition.
  3. Risk Assessment: Based on the analysis, the extension calculates a risk score and confidence level to determine the likelihood of the site being a phishing attempt.
  4. User Protection: If a high-risk site is detected, Kavach AI immediately alerts you and provides options to proceed safely.

All analysis is performed with privacy in mind, ensuring that sensitive data never leaves your device unnecessarily.
